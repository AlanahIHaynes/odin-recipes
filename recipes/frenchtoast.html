Delicious French Toast Recipe

Tasty French Toast
This recipe tells you how to make the tastiest, fluffiest French Toast to start your family's day off right. It only takes 10 minutes!
Scroll down to see the recipe.

Ingredients

  • 8 Large Slices Bread
  • 4 Large Eggs
  • 1 Cup Whole Milk
  • 1 Tablespoon Sugar
  • 1 Teaspoon Vanilla Extract
  • 1/2 Teaspoon Cinnamon
  • Pinch Salt
  • 2 Tablespoons Butter

Steps

  1. In a shallow bowl, whish eggs, milk, vanilla, sugar, cinnamon, and a pinch of salt until fully combined.
  2. Preheat your skillet over medium-low heat. Melt the butter in the pan.
  3. Dip a slice of bread into the egg mixture, allowing a few seconds for the egg to soak into the bread. Flip the bread over and repeat on the other side.
  4. Place the bread in the pan and cook over medium-low heat until cooked through. This will take about 4 minutes per side. Continue to add butter as needed for the remaining slices of bread.
