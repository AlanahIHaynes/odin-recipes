Ham & Swiss Breakfast Omelette Recipe

Best Ham & Swiss Omelette


This recipe tells you to create the most delicious, tasty and flavorful Ham & Swiss Egg Omelette. It is super easy to make; only takes about 10 minutes total. Serves 1. Enjoy, my breakfast lovers!
Scroll below for the recipe.

Ingredients

  • 3 Large Eggs
  • 1 Tablespoon Water
  • 3 Tablespoon Ham
  • 2 Slices Swiss Cheese
  • 1 Tablespoon Butter
  • Salt and Pepper to taste

Steps

  1. In a large bowl, beat the eggs and water until fully mixed. Stir in the ham and put aside.
  2. Add the butter to your skillet and allow it to fully melt.
  3. Add the whisked eggs to the skillet and cook on medium-low heat. Do not stir.
  4. Once the edges begin setting, push the edges to the center so it gets fluffy. This is to add volume.
  5. Add the swiss cheese and season with salt and pepper. Carefully fold the omelette in half. Turn off the heat. Enjoy while hot.
