Boiled Egg Sandwich Recipe

Delicious Boiled Egg Sandwich


This recipe will yield 4 servings of the most delicious egg sandwich! It uses household ingredients like bread, mayo, mustard and eggs, so you can make it at your convenience! Hope you enjoy!
Scroll down below to see the recipe.

Ingredients

  • 2 Large, Hard-Boiled Eggs
  • 2 Tablespoons Mayonnaise
  • 1 Tablespoon Mustard
  • 1 Tablespoon Chives
  • Salt and Black Pepper (to taste)
  • 2 Slices Preferred Bread (White Bread is recommended)

Steps

  1. Make the egg salad by chopping up the boiled egg with a fork. Mix the hard boiled egg, mayonnaise and mustard together. Sprinkle with salt and pepper to taste. Mix with a spoon.
  2. Assemble the sandwich. Toast your bread slices. Spread the egg mixture on top of one slice.
  3. Store leftovers in a sealed container in the refrigerator. Good for up to 3 days.
